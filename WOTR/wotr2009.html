2009 War of the Ring Online Tournament

Rules Issues Clarifications for the Tournament

The following are several rule issues that have come to my attention, I will list them with my opinion of correct resolution, I invite conversation on the topics although the three official judges have final say.

The Free rolling event dice before Shadow has finished allocating eyes: The Free must reroll. This was clearly discussed in the general discussion thread before the tournament. Free must wait until Shadow has completed their eye allocation and, to be safe, rolled the Shadow event dice.

A player rolling combat dice before opponent allowed to play combat card: If the player who rolled did not play a combat card then the opportunity to play the card is forfeited. If the opponent was not given a chance to play his card before one player begins rolling then I think the roll should be cancelled and the opponent allowed to play the card. We cannot allow the roll to stand since there are cards that limit the number of dice rolled.

WK special card draw on first round of combat: I asked a similar question in the general discussion before the tournament, we need to reach a consensus on this. When is it too late to draw for the WK? I think it is too late when cards have been played for the next combat round (or dice rolled) or when your opponent has declared the event die he will use for his next action. Hitting escape to kill the die and cards could also be used as a trigger, but I would favor the more lenient approach.
